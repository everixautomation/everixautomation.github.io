// how it works

The Everix Intake System

Five automated layers that work together to capture, qualify, and convert more of your inbound leads — without adding headcount.

01

Instant Lead Capture

When a prospect submits your contact form, calls your number, or messages you at any hour, our system responds within 60 seconds. No more leads waiting until Monday morning. First contact is made while they're still thinking about your firm — before they've Googled the next option.

02

AI-Powered Intake Screening

Our AI asks the right questions: practice area, case details, jurisdiction, urgency, and budget signals. Only qualified, viable leads are forwarded to your team — saving senior lawyers from 90-minute consultations that go nowhere. The AI knows your firm's intake criteria intimately.

03

Automated Consultation Booking

Qualified leads book directly into your calendar — no phone tag, no back-and-forth email. The system syncs with your existing calendar, sends confirmation and reminders, and reduces no-shows by up to 40%.

04

Smart Follow-Up Sequences

Leads that don't book immediately receive a structured follow-up cadence — SMS and email — over 7 days. Each message is timed and personalized based on where they are in the decision process. We recover the "I'll think about it" leads that currently disappear.

05

Real-Time Reporting Dashboard

See every lead that came in, how they were handled, what was said, and what happened next. Full transparency, complete audit trail, and clear ROI metrics — so you always know what your intake system is worth.

Law firm data is sensitive. Every component of the Everix system is designed to meet PIPEDA requirements and Law Society expectations.

🔒

PIPEDA-Compliant Data Handling

All prospective client data is collected, stored, and processed in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act. No data sold, shared, or used for any purpose other than your firm's intake.

🇨🇦

Canadian Data Residency

Where required, data can be stored on Canadian servers. We work with your firm's IT and compliance team to ensure requirements are met for your jurisdiction and Law Society.

📋

Conflict Check Integration

Our intake flow can be configured to collect the information your team needs to run a conflict check before a consultation is confirmed — ensuring no privileged information is exchanged prematurely.

🛡️

Privilege-Aware Design

Everix does not provide legal advice or opinions. Our AI clearly identifies itself as an automated intake assistant — maintaining the distinction between AI-assisted intake and solicitor-client communication.
