How it works

The Everix Intake System

Five automated layers that capture, qualify, and convert more of your inbound leads — without adding headcount.

01

Instant Lead Capture

When a prospect submits your contact form, calls, or messages at any hour, our system responds within 60 seconds. First contact made while they're still thinking about your firm — before they've Googled the next option.

02

AI-Powered Intake Screening

Our AI asks the right questions: practice area, case details, jurisdiction, urgency, and budget signals. Only qualified, viable leads are forwarded to your team — saving senior lawyers from unproductive consultations.

03

Automated Consultation Booking

Qualified leads book directly into your calendar — no phone tag, no back-and-forth. Syncs with your existing calendar, sends confirmations and reminders, and reduces no-shows by up to 40%.

04

Smart Follow-Up Sequences

Leads that don't book immediately receive a structured 7-day follow-up cadence — SMS and email — timed and personalized based on where they are in the decision process. We recover the "I'll think about it" leads that currently disappear.

05

Real-Time Reporting Dashboard

See every lead, how they were handled, what was said, and what happened next. Full transparency, complete audit trail, and clear ROI metrics so you always know what your intake system is worth.
